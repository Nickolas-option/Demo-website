Methodology

All stability metrics below read precomputed values from run.json and core_output.json. Scale-normalized means the score shift is divided by score scale span.

Metric Meaning Formula
Score Instability How much the score changes when the same criterion is paraphrased. same-criterion MAD / scale span
Ranking Stability Whether example ordering stays consistent when the same criterion is paraphrased. mean same-criterion Kendall tau-b
Flips Estimated share of ranking pairs that reverse order after same-criterion paraphrasing. same-criterion discordant pairs / same-criterion total ranking pairs
Spillover Whether paraphrasing one criterion changes scores on other criteria. cross MAD / same-criterion MAD
